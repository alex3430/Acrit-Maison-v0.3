Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

At Acrit Maison, we collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when placing an order, including your name, phone number, email address, and delivery address. This information is essential for processing and delivering your orders.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process your orders, communicate with you about your purchases, improve our services, and send you updates about new collections and offers (with your consent).

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All data is encrypted during transmission and stored securely. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary for order fulfillment.

4. Cookies

Our website uses local storage to maintain your shopping cart and wishlist preferences. No tracking cookies are used without your consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Contact us at privacy@acritmaison.com for any data-related requests.

3. Pricing

All prices are listed in Bangladeshi Taka (BDT/৳). Prices are subject to change without prior notice. The price at the time of order placement will be the final price charged.

Orders are confirmed upon successful submission of the checkout form. We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) for all orders within Bangladesh. Additional payment methods will be available soon.

Standard delivery within Bangladesh takes 3–5 business days. International orders take 7–14 business days. Shipping fees are calculated based on the order total. Orders above ৳100,000 qualify for free shipping within Bangladesh.
